Kitchen Hacks

Quick Garlic Peeling

To easily peel garlic cloves, place them in a small bowl and shake vigorously for a few seconds.

Sharp Knife

Keep your knives sharp. A sharp knife is safer and more efficient than a dull one.

Vinegar for Cleaning

Use vinegar to clean and deodorize your microwave. Heat a bowl of vinegar in the microwave for a couple of minutes, then wipe down the interior.

Lemon for Odor Removal

Use lemon juice to remove odors from cutting boards and your hands after handling strong-smelling foods like garlic and onions.

Rubber Bands on Jars

Wrap rubber bands around stubborn jar lids to provide better grip for opening.

Organization Hacks

Drawer Dividers

Use cardboard or plastic dividers to keep your drawers organized and items separated.

Cabinet Door Storage

Install hooks or racks on the inside of cabinet doors to hang pot lids, measuring cups, or small baskets for spices.

Stackable Storage Containers

Invest in stackable storage containers to maximize shelf space and keep dry goods like flour, sugar, and grains organized.

Labeling

Label jars, containers, and shelves to easily identify ingredients and keep everything in its place.

Magnetic Strips

Install magnetic strips on the wall to hold knives and metal utensils, freeing up counter and drawer space.

Technology Hacks

Phone Stand

Make a phone stand using a paperclip by bending it into a shape that holds your phone securely.


Think you want better hacks than paper clips holding your phone, here are some juicy hacks

Keyboard Shortcuts

Learn and utilize keyboard shortcuts for your computer and mobile devices to navigate and perform tasks more quickly.

Dual Monitors

If you work on a computer for extended periods, consider using dual monitors to increase productivity by allowing you to view multiple windows or applications simultaneously.

Screen Recording

Use built-in screen recording features or third-party screen recording software to create tutorials, capture gameplay, or demonstrate software issues.

Distraction Blockers

Install distraction-blocking apps or browser extensions like Freedom or StayFocusd to limit access to distracting websites and apps during work or study sessions.

Health Hacks

Posture Improvement

Place a small pillow behind your lower back when sitting to maintain good posture and reduce strain.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated. Carry a reusable water bottle with you to make it easier to drink water regularly.

Meal Prep

Spend some time each week meal prepping to ensure you have healthy options readily available, which can help you make better food choices and avoid unhealthy fast food or convenience options.

Limit Processed Foods

Minimize your intake of processed and packaged foods high in added sugars, unhealthy fats, and sodium, which can contribute to various health problems when consumed in excess.

Limit Screen Time

Reduce screen time and take regular breaks from electronic devices to give your eyes and mind a rest, and to encourage more physical activity.
